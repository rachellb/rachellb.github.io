Rachel Bennett, PhD

Postdoctoral Research Associate

Lewis Katz School of Medicine at Temple University

Philadelphia, PA

I am a Postdoctoral Research Associate at the Lewis Katz School of Medicine at Temple University. I earned my PhD in Industrial Engineering from the University of Oklahoma, where my dissertation, Toward Trustworthy Machine Learning in Precision Health: Interpretable, Scalable, and Fair Methods for Clinical Prediction, focused on machine learning, big data analytics, and predictive modeling for healthcare and bioinformatics.

My research develops scalable, interpretable, and fair machine learning methods for clinical prediction. Current applications include maternal health, length-of-stay prediction, and identifying suboptimal follow-up care among childhood cancer survivors.
