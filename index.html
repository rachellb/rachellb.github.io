I am a PhD candidate in Industrial Engineering at the University of Oklahoma, specializing in machine learning, big data analytics, and predictive modeling with a focus on healthcare and bioinformatics. My research aims to develop scalable and interpretable machine learning models to address critical challenges in precision medicine.

With a strong background in data science and analytics, I have contributed to numerous publications and presentations, showcasing innovative solutions for early prediction of medical conditions such as preeclampsia and cesarean delivery. My work is driven by a passion for leveraging high-performance computing to create trustworthy and impactful algorithms.
